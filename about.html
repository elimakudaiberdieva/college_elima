Frequently Asked Questions

What are the entry requirements for engineering programs?

Minimum requirements include high school diploma with 75% in Math and Science subjects, and qualifying entrance exam scores. International students need equivalent qualifications and English proficiency test scores.

Are internships included in the programs?

Yes, all programs include mandatory internships. We have partnerships with 200+ companies for summer internships and co-op placements.

What scholarship opportunities are available?

We offer merit-based scholarships (up to 100% tuition), need-based financial aid, sports scholarships, and special scholarships for women in engineering.
